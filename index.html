Literacy rate of Metpur

Literacy rate in Metpur town area is 79%. 1879 out of total 2504 population is educated here. Among males the literacy rate is 84% as 1083 males out of total 1354 are educated however female literacy rate is 74% as 816 out of total 1103 females are educated in this Town Area. The dark part is that illiteracy rate of Metpur town area is 20%. Here 501 out of total 2504 individuals are illiterate. Male illiteracy rate here is 15% as 270 males out of total 1354 are illiterate. In females the illiteracy rate is 25% and 275 out of total 1103 females are illiterate in this town area.

Agricultural status of Metpur

The count of working individual of Metpur town area is 1048 whereas 1409 are un-employed. And out of 1048 employed individual 54 individuals are fully dependent on cultivation.
